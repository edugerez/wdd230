Contrast

Fender company
fender.com

In this screenshot of fender musical instruments, we can see how the contrast used enhances the visual hierarchy towards the guitar. Contrast as a principle helps the presentation have a visual meaning for those who enter the page. the black background as a base and the white letters generate a perfect reading contrast as well as the contrast between the image, simple buttons, and legible letters. all attention is directed to the product that is trying to sell in this case.

Alignment

Tesla company
tesla.com

In this example of the famous Tesla cars, we see that the page is fully aligned in all its elements, but in turn the entire alignment is centered in the image of the car and in more detail in the car itself, both the buttons below and the title of the car model making a relationship through the alignment, which is what you want show as parent. alignment is the invisible detail that makes all the difference to show organization, visual harmony and clean structural design.

Proximity

HBOmax company
HBOmax.com

Here we see that proximity is used in the main and large letters within the video that is being played, this approximation subtly conveys to us: if you want to see these incredible movies, this is only what you must pay per month! Proximity is the closeness in how the elements are grouped denoting a relationship between themselves and the non-relationship when the closeness is not visible. This approach, I believe, often helps to make navigation both in pages and in applications more intuitive and therefore a better experience for the user.
