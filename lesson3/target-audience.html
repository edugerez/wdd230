Audience

The website is designed to attract regional companies, providers and small or big business. The page provides information, and generate contacts net both at regional level and national level. At the same time, it seeks to capture an audience whose might be seeking information about promotion, rates, legal requirements and even tursitic regional information. The audience will access the site through desktop and mobile devices but not through an application. Every visitor will have the option to register as a associate commerce or provider with specific requirments.

Persona

It is expected as personas adult people focused on commerce and net working regional groups who joing to improve and strenght their business, most of them are owners of companies, representants of those companies and members who are seeking for infomations and oportunities of contacts, capacitation and news related with commerce issues on the region.
