Purpose

The purpose of the Villa Mercedes Chamber of Commerce is to carry out various activities to promote the development and strengthening of local companies and provide various services for the benefit of members both as merchants and suppliers. On this page you will find legal information, you will seek to generate contact networks, receive notifications of training, register for contests, achieve beneficial agreements for the members of the chamber. Each member of the chamber as well as visitors in general will be able to receive advice for the development of their companies as well as information to delve into local marketing. The main purpose of the page is to connect merchants, provide information and arrange meetings to seek benefits for regional development.
